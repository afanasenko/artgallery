Evgeniy Titov was born in 1963 in Shchigry village, Siberia.
Education: I. E. Repin state academy institute of painting, sculpture and architecture, 1992 – 1997, faculty of painting.

Exhibitions

1999 – 2008 – individual and group exhibitions in the Central house of artists, Moscow.
2002 – International exhibition of small forms, Barcelona.
2002 – Still-life painting exhibition, Union of artists, Saint Petersburg.
2005 – Participating in “Hecatomb for Rembrandt” exhibition, Union of artists, Saint Petersburg.
